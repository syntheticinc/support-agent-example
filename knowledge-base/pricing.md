# Acme Pricing Plans

Acme offers three plans:

- **Free** — $0/month. Up to 3 projects, up to 5 users, 5 GB storage, community support only.
- **Pro** — $29 per user/month. Unlimited projects, up to 50 users, 100 GB storage, email support.
- **Business** — $99 per user/month. Unlimited projects, unlimited users, 1 TB storage, SSO, priority support, dedicated customer success manager.

Annual billing gives a 20% discount. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from Settings -> Billing.
