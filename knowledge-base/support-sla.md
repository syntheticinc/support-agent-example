# Support and Response Times

Support response-time SLAs by plan:

- **Free** — community forum only, no guaranteed response time.
- **Pro** — email support with a **24-hour** response-time SLA.
- **Business** — priority support with a **4-hour** response-time SLA, plus a dedicated customer success manager.

Support hours are Monday to Friday. Business-plan customers also get weekend coverage for urgent incidents.
